Gateau Breton

aka Jam-Filled Shortbread Tart

Be transformed to another place with the this decadent pastry. Made with European butter and a fruit jam for you liking, this perfect companion to you morning cup of coffee or tea will allow you to relax and savory the interplay of rich butter and sweet jam.

This tart is a perfect recipe for wowing you dinner party guests or a gift to your hostess or host. They will think you bought it from a boulangerie, while you impress them by saying that you make it with your own two hands. Ina Garten would be very proud
