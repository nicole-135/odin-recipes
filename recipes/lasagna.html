Best Lasagna Ever

Description

This lasagna is a family favorite and is asked for by my husband and his family. Enjoy the abundance of cheese (ricotta, mozzarella and provolone to be exact) as you cut a hearty slice to serve with a nice side salad or a piece of warm focaccia bread.

Enjoy the decadent mixture of sausage, ground beef, tomatoes and fresh herbs that make this sinful yet light American Italian fare.
